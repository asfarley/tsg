In this package you will find a valuable collection of vehicle models to feed your needs!

Every car has its own prefab, and its own material, exeption made for the car's glass and signs who share the same material.

Every car's material has 3 textures:

-DIFFUSE
-AO
-SPEC (metallic\smoothness workflow)

The nomenclature for the meshes are the following:

-TRAFFIC_XXX Body
-TRAFFIC_XXX Glass
-TRAFFIC_XXX Signs
-TRAFFIC_XXX WFL (W:wheel F:front L:left)
-TRAFFIC_XXX WFR (wheel front right)
-TRAFFIC_XXX WRL (wheel rear left)
-TRAFFIC_XXX WRR (wheel rear right)

Have fun!
